Hello,
       Welcome to my 100-year digital clock!
       When I asked myself what would change as time passes, the first thing that came to my mind is hand. You can easily read a person’s life through her hand as it grows larger and get wrinkles. Different gestures can also reflect different emotions and inner growth. Therefore, hands become the key element of my clock design.
       In this digital clock, the basic unit of time is not second or minute, but a year, and the hand that appears in the center of the page will age and change every year to represent how time goes by. Moreover, there is a personal story behind each hand that I would like to share.The small images at the right of the page are the hands of past years. You can click on one of those images to see more details.

       Come back every year to check out the new hand and the new story! If you would like to receive update notice of the new hand every year, please put your email address into the email box at the bottom of the page to subscribe this clock.
