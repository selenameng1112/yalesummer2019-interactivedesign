When I was 18 years old, despite the refusals I received, I insisted to visit one after another museum with my proposal about Virtual Reality Museum and the belief that every student,regardless of background, should receive the opportunity to education. ​ I aim to develop a​ Virtual Reality Art Museum Alliance Program​ which displays VR museums of America's top universities. It will provide students accessible and low-cost educational field trips to university museums and will deepen their understanding of art and history in a truly engaging way anytime and anywhere.

当我 18 岁的时候，在雷雨般的拒绝中，我依旧抱着研究报告、提案，和教育面前人人平等的信念，坚定地走访了一座又一座博物馆。为了让全世界的贫困地区的孩子都能够通过互联网感受到这最顶尖学校的艺术博物馆的熏陶, 筹建一个由普林斯顿大学艺术馆，耶鲁 大学艺术馆等组成的虚拟艺术博物馆联盟。

When I was 17 years old, I went on a service trip to Shanxi as a volunteer teacher. At the school,I was deeply touched by those little angels’ thirst for knowledge and the outside world. I also suddenly realized that although volunteer teaching let them see the sunlight, it didn't really help them open the door to the outside world. When we parted, I wiped their tears and held their hands firmly, determining to provide them with more access to knowledge through my efforts.

当我 17 岁的时候，我来到山西支教。我被学校里的小天使们眼中对外面世界和知识的渴望深深感动。我也突然意识到，支教虽然让他们看到阳光，但并没有真正帮助他们打开通往外面世界的门。离别时，我擦干他们的眼泪，紧握着他们的手，下定决心要通过自己的努力改变他们求知的世界。

When I was 16 years old, I found strength in dancing. I established the first dance team at our school and held dance class every week. I led my team to finish one after another performances in enthusiastic applause, and became more and more confident during this process.

当我 16 岁的时候，我在舞蹈中找到了生命和活力。因为对舞蹈对热爱，我组建了学校的第一支舞蹈团，并每周开设舞蹈课教同学跳舞。我带领着舞蹈队在同学老师的尖叫和掌声中完成一次次精彩的表演，变得越来越大方而自信。

当我 15 岁的时候，我挥手告别了父母，怀着对未知的忐忑和憧憬，独自一人穿越半个地球来到美国读高中读书，开启了我追寻真理的征尘。也是在那一年，我学会了在困难面前拍拍自己的后背鼓励自己，在擦干眼泪以后露出乐观坚定的笑容。

When I was 14 years old, I started to play tennis. At first, I was not confident because of my skills. However, I determined to always play better than yesterday. When I was 13, a family trip to Greece, Turkey and Russia expand my horizon. I was eager to continue to explore different cultures all over the world.

当我 13 岁的时候，一次希腊、土耳其和俄罗斯的家庭旅行，改变了我对世界的认知，触摸的蓝色清真寺的那一刹那，我决心去继续探索和破解全世界不同地域的不同文化。

When I was 12 years old, with my camera, a microphone and great enthusiasm, I led my classmates to visit the Central Axis of Beijing to find the historical imprint and connotation of Chinese culture. Our enthusiasm and research attracted more and more people to join us. We were even interviewed and reported by Beijing Television.

当我12岁的时候，我拿起了相机，话筒，还有满腔热情，带领着同学走访了北京中轴线，去寻找中国文化的历史印记和内涵精神。我们的热情和研究吸引了一个又一个路人加入我们，最后甚至被北京市电台采访和报道。

When I was 9 years old, my friends and I spread pamphlets made by ourselves to call on people to protect water resources on the street. We even invited people to write down their determination to protect our environment on hydrogen balloons, and then released them into the air together. Although we didn’t know how much changes our actions could bring, we were still happy for making a contribution to our community.

当我 9岁的时候，我和同学自发地拿着自己一张张手绘的宣传卡片在街道上呼吁大家保护水资源。我们邀请大家在氢气球上写下自己对保护资源的承诺，再一起将气球和决心放飞到空中。虽然不知道我们的行动能带来多少改变，但还是纯粹地为自己可以为社会作出贡 献而感到快乐。
